Campaign Process

The systematic approach I take with every client campaign, from brief to reporting.

01

Discovery & Goal Setting

Understand your business, audience, competitors, and define clear KPIs before touching the ads manager.

02

Audience & Keyword Research

Build detailed audience segments, research high-intent keywords, and map the customer journey.

03

Creative Strategy

Brief copy and creatives aligned to each funnel stage — awareness, consideration, and conversion.

04

Campaign Setup & Launch

Structure campaigns with proper tracking, pixel events, UTMs, and conversion goals configured.

05

Test & Optimise

Daily monitoring, A/B testing, audience pruning, and bid strategy adjustments to improve efficiency.

06

Reporting & Scale

Weekly reports with clear insights. Scale winning ad sets while pausing losers to protect your budget.
