My Story

Turning Clicks Into
Business Results

I'm Aman Kumar Pathak — a Performance Marketing Specialist with an MBA in Marketing & Operations. My focus is simple: help businesses grow through smart, data-driven digital marketing that delivers a real return on ad spend.

I got into digital marketing because I was fascinated by the intersection of psychology, data, and creativity. Every ad is a hypothesis — a message to a specific audience, at a specific moment. The job is to test, learn, and optimise until the numbers back you up.

My expertise spans Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram), Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube), Social Media Management, Lead Generation, and Content Strategy. I've worked with brands across real estate, e-commerce, education, and services verticals.

Beyond the ads manager, I bring a business-owner mindset to every engagement. I care about your CPL, ROAS, and conversion rate — not just impressions and reach. Every campaign I build starts with understanding your customers and ends with numbers you can take to the bank.
